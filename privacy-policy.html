Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using this bot, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect & Why

We only collect the information necessary for the bot to function properly:

  • User IDs & Server IDs – Used to save level progress, moderation history, and server settings.
  • Message activity – Required for the leveling system to calculate XP and levels.
  • Moderation actions – To track warnings, timeouts, kicks, and bans within the server.
  • Server events – Logged for moderation and auditing purposes (e.g., member joins/leaves, message edits/deletes, role changes, voice activity).

This information is used exclusively to provide the bot's services, including leveling, moderation, logging, and utility features.

2. How We Protect Your Data

Your data is stored securely in JSON files on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee against all potential risks, including data loss or breaches.

3. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties.
  • Use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • Collect data beyond what is necessary for the bot to function.

Data may be accessed by authorized administrators only when needed to maintain the bot or resolve issues.

4. Your Control Over Your Data

  • Removing the bot from your server automatically deletes all associated data.
  • You can view your personal data (e.g., XP, moderation history) using the bot's commands.
  • You can request the removal of your data by contacting your server administrator or via email (see Section 7).

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the bot's services. Once the bot is removed from a server, all stored data for that server and its members is permanently deleted.

6. Your Choices

  • You can choose to limit your interactions with the bot. Note that some features (like leveling or logging) may not work fully if data collection is restricted.
  • You can opt out of certain notifications (e.g., DM warnings) where possible.

7. Contact & Questions

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, data access, or deletion requests:

  • Contact your server administrator first.
  • For further inquiries, email us at: toquimos.gg@gmail.com

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
